Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build and ship? expand_more
Most standard builds take 3-5 business days for assembly, BIOS flashing, and 24-hour stress testing. Shipping anywhere in India takes an additional 2-4 days via our secure logistics partners.
What if a part arrives damaged? expand_more
We use custom wooden crates and internal foam packaging for all fully built PCs. If damage occurs during transit, our 100% transit insurance covers it. Contact support within 24 hours of delivery.
Can I upgrade my PC later? expand_more
Absolutely! We use standard non-proprietary parts. You can upgrade any component in the future without voiding our labor warranty on the original parts.
Do you provide a warranty for the PCs? expand_more
Yes! All MakeMyPC builds come with a standard 3-year labor warranty. Individual components carry their own manufacturer warranties, which typically range from 3 to 10 years depending on the part.
Are the Windows OS and drivers pre-installed? expand_more
Yes, if you select an OS during your build configuration, we will install it along with all the latest motherboard, GPU, and chipset drivers. Your PC arrives fully plug-and-play ready out of the box.
Do you offer financing or EMI options? expand_more
Yes! We partner with major banks and finance providers to offer No-Cost EMIs on credit cards, as well as cardless EMI options through Bajaj Finserv and ZestMoney at checkout.
